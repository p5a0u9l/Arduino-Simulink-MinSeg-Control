Part a¶

Let state variable $\mathbf{x}$ be \begin{equation} \mathbf{x} = \begin{vmatrix} \alpha \\ \dot{\alpha} \\ x \\ \dot{x} \\ \end{vmatrix} = \begin{vmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \\ x_4 \\ \end{vmatrix} \end{equation}

Solve using Python and SymPy package¶

# elements of Eq 1
M1 = Matrix([[-(Ip + mp*L**2), mp*L*cos(alpha)], [mp*L*rw**2*cos(alpha), -(Iw+mw*rw**2+mp*rw**2)]])
